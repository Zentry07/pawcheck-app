Privacy Policy

PawCheck — Pet Food Safety Analyzer

Effective Date: March 3, 2026

Introduction

PawCheck ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS mobile application and related services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (for authentication and account management)
  • Account credentials through Apple Sign-In

Pet Profile Information

  • Pet name and species (e.g., dog, cat)
  • Breed
  • Weight
  • Known allergies and dietary restrictions

Usage Data

  • Scan history (food products you analyze)
  • Chat messages with our AI nutrition assistant
  • Interaction logs with the app's features

Subscription Information

  • Subscription status and purchase history (managed by RevenueCat)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain app functionality and services
  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • To analyze pet food products against your pet's profile
  • To power our AI-driven nutrition chat assistant
  • To manage subscriptions and process payments
  • To improve our app, services, and user experience
  • To send service-related notifications and support messages
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

Supabase

We use Supabase for authentication and secure data storage. Supabase stores your account credentials, pet profiles, and scan history. Supabase has its own privacy practices—please review their Privacy Policy for details.

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and process in-app purchases. RevenueCat handles payment information and subscription status. Please review RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for details on their data practices.

Apple Sign-In

We use Apple Sign-In for authentication. Apple does not share your email with us unless you allow it. Please review Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

Anthropic Claude AI

We use Anthropic's Claude AI to power our nutrition chat assistant. Your chat messages and pet profile data are sent to Anthropic's API to generate personalized nutrition insights. Please review Anthropic's Privacy Policy for details on how they process data.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We only share information with third-party service providers as necessary to operate the app and provide its services (Supabase, RevenueCat, and Anthropic).

We may disclose your information if required by law, to protect our legal rights, or to respond to lawful government requests.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data as long as your account is active and necessary to provide services. If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal information from our systems within 30 days, except where required by law to retain records.

You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at support@pawcheck.app.

6. Children's Privacy

PawCheck is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information and terminate the child's account.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption and secure data storage through Supabase. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of certain data uses

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@pawcheck.app.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy in the app or sending you an email. Your continued use of PawCheck following the posting of revised Privacy Policy means you accept and agree to the changes.
